Cat Breeds

Cats come in all shapes and sized but broadly they are divided into 3 distinct groups: domesticated, wild and imaginary. Lets look at each of them in turn!

Domesticated

Ragdoll

The Ragdoll cat is possibly the most laid back of all the domestic cat breeds. These cats are basically content and undemanding and tolerate most situations.

Read more (about ragdoll breed)
Abyssinian

The Abyssinian cat is quiet, intelligent and curious and is said to enjoy human company and become attached to its family.

Read more (about abyssinian breed)
Domestic shorthaired

Animal Planet affectionately refers to Domestic shorthair cats as the mutts of the cat world because they're a mix of various breeds, resulting in a vast range of markings, colors, and personalities.
